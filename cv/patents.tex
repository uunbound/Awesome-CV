    {
      \begin{cvitems}
        \item {Algorithms for detecting the reuse of information across documents and attributing credit back to original sources.}
        \item {The method recursively identifies chains of reused content (regardless of paraphrasing or changes in authorship), apportions value to original contributors using a provably fair economic attribution model that distinguishes value from quantity, and includes computational shortcuts to make the approach practical at scale.}
